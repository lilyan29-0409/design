Jo designer I’m a product designer who strives to create experiences that feel clear, thoughtful, and genuinely helpful. I find inspiration in everyday life—through cooking, photography, and camping, where small details often speak the loudest. Winter is my favorite season; its cold, crisp air brings a kind of calm that I try to reflect in my work. Meditation and yoga help me stay grounded, allowing me to approach design with balance, intention, and empathy. Above all, I’m committed to delivering meaningful, user-centered design that makes people feel supported at every step.
